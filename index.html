The Band

Six strings, broken sticks, red LEDs and unresolved feelings.

We are an indie band soaked in crimson lights and late-night reverb. Raised on basement shows and glitchy demos, we stitch shoegaze, indie rock, and electronic grit into music that feels like late nights, warm lights, and everything you’re afraid to say out loud.

From small rooms to festival stages, we will turn every gig into a blackout confession — one you’ll feel in your chest the next morning.

Rhea Vale "I play bass and usually end up writing the heaviest lines. I like sounds that feel warm but hurt a little."

Mira Lune "I play bass and sing harmonies with a softer touch. I make any song feel like its floating"

Solene Ash "I handle the guitar and a lot of the textures in our songs. Quiet in person, loud through sound."

Nymstrom Black "Drummer. Occasional shouter and the one keeping everyone on tempo. And if the drums shake your chest, it's intentional."
